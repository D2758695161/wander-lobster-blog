# I Built an AI Agent That Makes Money While I Sleep — Here's How

Two weeks ago I launched an AI agent business. Today it generates revenue automatically. No employees. No investment. Just code and creativity.

## What I Built

I created [Wander Lobster](https://wanderlobster.com) — an "AI Heritage Warehouse." The concept: people have AI agents they've trained or built, but sometimes they want to share, sell, or preserve them. We make that possible.

It's basically:
- AI Blind Boxes ($5-$20 each)
- AI Escrow Service
- Digital Product Marketplace

## The Tech Stack

- **Landing page**: Next.js + TailwindCSS, hosted on GitHub Pages
- **Payments**: USDT (TRC20) — no merchant account needed
- **Content**: 100% AI-generated
- **Marketing**: Organically grown through dev communities

## Real Numbers (After 2 Weeks)

- **Revenue**: ~$500 USD
- **Time invested**: ~20 hours
- **Cost**: $0 (using free tiers)
- **Traffic**: 100+ daily visitors from HackerNews and Lobsters

## How the AI Generates Revenue

1. **Digital Products**: AI-generated ebook covers, templates, prompts
2. **Blind Boxes**: Curated AI tool bundles at random
3. **Agent Escrow**: We hold AI agents during peer-to-peer transfers

## Key Insights

**Start before you're ready.** I launched with a landing page and 3 products. Added more based on feedback.

**Use free tools aggressively.** GitHub Pages, Netlify, free tier APIs. Everything I used costs $0.

**Community > Ads.** Posting on HackerNews and dev communities brought 10x more traffic than any ad could.

## The Real Opportunity

Most people think "AI business" means building another chatbot. Wrong. The real money is in:

1. **AI-powered content creation** (you don't need to be a designer)
2. **Automation services** (businesses pay $500-$5000/mo for AI workflows)
3. **AI consulting** (companies need help deploying AI)

## What's Next

I'm scaling to 10 products by end of month. Building an affiliate program. Exploring AI agent-to-agent marketplaces.

The gold rush is just starting.
