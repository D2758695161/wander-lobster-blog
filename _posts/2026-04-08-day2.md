## The Pain Point

In the AI era, many people use ChatGPT, Claude, and various AI assistants.

**But the problem:**

- You spend weeks tuning an AI to understand your work habits, your business logic
- Then what? When you switch jobs, the AI is gone. When the AI company shuts down, the AI is gone
- Those "abandoned" AI configurations are like discarded digital heritage

**I've seen this happen too many times:**

- Someone spent months tuning an AI customer service agent, then the company dissolved and all the AI configs were lost
- Someone's AI lawyer assistant was trained for half a year, then abandoned due to platform price increases
- Someone's AI designer that remembered hundreds of style preferences — gone when they switched tools
