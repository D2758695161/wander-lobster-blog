## You Would Never Abandon Your Best Employee. Why Abandon Your Best AI?

Think about what happens at work:

- You spend 3 months training a new employee. They learn your business, your preferences, your systems. Then you leave the job. The employee stays.

- But with AI — you spend 3 months tuning your AI assistant. It learns your coding style, your business logic, your workflow. Then you switch tools, or the platform shuts down, or you just want a fresh start. The AI is gone.

**This is what we call "AI heritage loss."**
