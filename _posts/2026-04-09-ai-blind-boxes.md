## Introducing AI Blind Boxes

A "blind box" is a concept from physical collectibles:
- You don't know exactly what's inside
- There's rarity (common, rare, legendary)
- There's the thrill of discovery

**AI blind boxes work the same way:**

1. Someone stores their AI on Wander Lobster
2. We clean it up, test it, categorize it
